La Plaza Tacos

Our tortillas are made using ancient nixtamalization techniques. We grind the corn fresh every morning to ensure that every taco has a soft, fragrant base that holds up to our juicy fillings.

The Al Pastor is our crown jewel. Marinated in achiote and pineapple juice, the pork is sliced thin and caramelized on the trompo until it reaches crispy perfection.

Flavor is our priority. We import our dried chilies directly from Oaxaca, creating complex moles and salsas that balance heat with deep, earthy sweetness.

Nothing pairs better with spicy salsa than our Horchata. Made with toasted rice, cinnamon, and a hint of vanilla, it’s the ultimate refreshing finish to your meal.
