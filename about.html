The Legacy of La Plaza

Our journey began in 1984 with a single grill and a passion for authentic flavors. Grandma Sanchez believed that food was the ultimate way to bring people together, and that philosophy remains our guiding light today.

We refuse to cut corners. While others use pre-packaged mixes, we still roast our own peppers and hand-squeeze our limes. The difference is in the details you can taste in every single bite.

Community is at the heart of everything we do. We source our produce from local growers, ensuring that our success helps support the local economy and provides you with the freshest ingredients possible.

Today, La Plaza Tacos is proud to be a staple in the neighborhood. Whether you're a first-timer or a regular who's been coming for decades, we welcome you to our table with open arms and a hot taco.
